About Us

Are you tired of staring at a bland, uninspired landscape every time you step outside? Let Leafy Escapes help you transform your outdoor space into a stunning natural oasis that you'll love spending time in. Our team of expert landscapers knows how to bring out the best in your property, with lush greenery, beautiful flowers, and thoughtfully designed hardscaping elements that will take your breath away.

Our Services

Landscape design and installation At Leafy Escapes, we offer a wide range of landscaping services to bring your outdoor space to life. Our expert team can design and install a landscape that is both beautiful and functional, ensuring that every detail is taken care of from start to finish. From tree and shrub planting to lawn care and maintenance, we can keep your yard looking its best year-round. We also specialize in irrigation system installation and repair, hardscaping, including patios, walkways, and retaining walls, as well as outdoor lighting design and installation.
